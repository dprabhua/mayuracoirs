Usage Instructions

Simple steps to get started with Baby Coir in Bales

1

Prepare the Bale

Place the baby coir bale in a suitable area and add water. Allow it to soak for 30-45 minutes until fully expanded.

2

Break and Mix

Break up the expanded coir and mix thoroughly with soil or other growing media as needed.

3

Ready to Use

Your baby coir is now ready for use in commercial agriculture, landscaping, or other large-scale applications.
