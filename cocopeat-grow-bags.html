Usage Instructions

Simple steps to get started with Cocopeat Grow Bags

1

Prepare the Bag

Place the grow bag in the desired location and add water. Allow it to soak for 30-45 minutes until fully expanded.

2

Plant and Grow

Once expanded, plant your crops directly in the grow bag. Provide appropriate care and watering schedule.

3

Maintain and Harvest

Monitor plant growth and provide necessary nutrients. Harvest when crops are ready.
