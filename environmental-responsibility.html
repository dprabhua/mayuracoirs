Environmental Responsibility & Safety

Our Commitment to Sustainable Manufacturing and Community Development

Sustainable Manufacturing Practices

At Mayuracoirs, we understand that the production of cocopeat and coco husk blocks, especially low EC varieties, requires careful attention to environmental impact. Our manufacturing processes are designed to minimize dust emissions and prevent salt-rich water from affecting the surrounding environment.

Dust Control

Advanced dust collection systems and enclosed processing areas ensure minimal environmental impact

Water Management

Efficient water treatment systems prevent salt-rich water from affecting local ecosystems

Waste Reduction

Comprehensive recycling programs minimize waste and maximize resource utilization

Community Development

As a rural-based manufacturing unit, we recognize our responsibility to contribute to the development of local communities. Our CSR initiatives focus on:

  • Educational programs for local schools
  • Health awareness campaigns
  • Knowledge sharing about sustainable agriculture
  • Skill development programs

Safety Information

We prioritize the safety of our employees, customers, and the environment. Our comprehensive safety program includes:

Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS)

We provide detailed MSDS documentation for all our products, containing essential information about:

  • Potential health hazards
  • Fire safety measures
  • Environmental impact
  • Safe handling procedures

Handling Guidelines

When working with our cocopeat products, we recommend:

  • Using standard agricultural safety equipment
  • Maintaining proper ventilation in storage areas
  • Following standard first aid procedures for any exposure
  • Implementing fire safety measures (though our products are non-flammable)

Product Life Cycle

Our compressed blocks maintain optimal quality for up to 100 days from production. We recommend breaking and hydrating the blocks within this period for best results.
