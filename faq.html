Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to Common Questions About Our Products and Services

What is the difference between High EC and Low EC products?

High EC (Electrical Conductivity) products have higher salt content and are typically used for general agricultural purposes. Low EC products have reduced salt content and are ideal for sensitive plants and seedlings.

How do I choose the right product for my needs?

The choice depends on your specific requirements. Consider factors like plant type, growth stage, and intended use. Our team can help you select the most suitable product for your needs.

What are the storage requirements for coir products?

Coir products should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep them in their original packaging until use to maintain quality.

What is your manufacturing process?

Our manufacturing process includes material preparation, washing, drying, and compression. Each stage is carefully monitored to ensure consistent quality and performance.

How do you ensure consistent product quality?

We implement strict quality control measures at every stage of production, including regular testing and monitoring of key parameters like EC, pH, and moisture content.

What quality certifications do you have?

We are ISO 9001:2015 certified and maintain various quality standards. Our products undergo regular testing to ensure they meet international quality requirements.

How do you test your products?

We conduct comprehensive testing including EC, pH, moisture content, and physical properties. Our in-house laboratory is equipped with modern testing equipment.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. Please contact our sales team for specific details based on your requirements.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship our products worldwide. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure safe and timely delivery.
