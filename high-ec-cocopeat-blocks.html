Usage Instructions

Simple steps to get started with High EC cocopeat blocks

1

Soak the Block

Place the cocopeat block in a container and add water. Allow it to soak for 30 minutes until fully expanded.

2

Break and Mix

Break up the expanded cocopeat and mix thoroughly. Add perlite or vermiculite for better aeration if needed.

3

Ready to Use

Your cocopeat is now ready for use in hydroponic systems, potting mixes, or as a soil amendment.
