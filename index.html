Our Products

Discover the versatility of our high-quality cocopeat blocks, designed for superior water retention, aeration, and nutrient-holding capabilities. Ideal for soil conditioning and as a growing medium for plants, our cocopeat blocks are perfect for both agricultural and horticultural uses.

Our products are 100% natural and organic, sourced from sustainable coconut husks. They are free from harmful chemicals and additives, ensuring a safe and eco-friendly solution for your gardening and farming needs.

Join us in our journey towards sustainability and explore the endless possibilities with Mayuracoirs.

Eco-Friendly Commitment

At Mayuracoirs, we are committed to promoting sustainability and environmental stewardship. Our manufacturing process is designed to minimize waste and reduce our carbon footprint. We believe in creating products that not only benefit our customers but also contribute positively to the environment.

Our cocopeat blocks are biodegradable and help in reducing soil erosion, improving soil structure, and enhancing the overall health of the ecosystem. By choosing Mayuracoirs, you are supporting a greener and more sustainable future.
