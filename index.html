Cocopeat Blocks

High-quality cocopeat blocks for superior water retention and aeration. Perfect for hydroponic systems, organic gardening, and soil conditioning. Our premium cocopeat is ideal for growing vegetables, flowers, and herbs in Tamil Nadu's climate. Features excellent moisture retention, natural pH balance, and sustainable sourcing.

Coir Pith

Premium coir pith for soil amendment and potting mixes. Enhances soil structure, improves water retention, and promotes healthy root development. Perfect for organic farming, landscaping, and sustainable agriculture practices. Our coir pith is processed to ensure consistent quality and optimal performance in various soil conditions.
