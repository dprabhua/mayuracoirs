Usage Instructions

Simple steps to get started with Low EC coco husk chip blocks

1

Soak the Block

Place the coco husk chip block in a container and add water. Allow it to soak for 30 minutes until fully expanded.

2

Break and Mix

Break up the expanded chips and mix thoroughly. Add perlite or vermiculite for better drainage if needed.

3

Ready to Use

Your coco husk chips are now ready for use in orchid cultivation, hydroponic systems, or specialized horticulture.
