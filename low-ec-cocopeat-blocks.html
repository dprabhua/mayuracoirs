Usage Instructions

Simple steps to get started with Low EC Cocopeat Blocks

1

Prepare the Block

Place the cocopeat block in a suitable container and add water. Allow it to soak for 30-45 minutes until fully expanded.

2

Break and Mix

Once expanded, break up the fibers and mix with soil or use as a standalone growing medium.

3

Plant and Grow

Use the prepared cocopeat for your plants. Provide appropriate care and watering schedule.
