Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

At Mayuracoirs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Contact information when you fill out our contact form
  • Basic analytics data (anonymized IP addresses, page views)
  • Information you provide when subscribing to our newsletter

Cookies and Tracking

We use first-party cookies only for essential website functionality:

  • Session cookies for maintaining your browsing session
  • Analytics cookies (first-party only) to understand website usage
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings

We do not use third-party cookies or tracking technologies.

Analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics that:

  • Anonymize IP addresses
  • Use first-party cookies only
  • Do not share data with third parties
  • Respect Do Not Track preferences

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
