Discover the Versatile Applications of Our Coir Products

Hydroponics

Our coir products are ideal for hydroponic systems, providing excellent water retention and aeration for optimal plant growth without soil.

Benefits

  • Superior water retention and drainage
  • Optimal root aeration
  • pH neutral and sterile medium
  • Sustainable alternative to rockwool
  • Reusable and biodegradable

Applications

  • Commercial hydroponic farming
  • Home hydroponic systems
  • Seed starting and propagation
  • Vegetable and herb production
